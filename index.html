Progress tracking begins at Level 31.

Levels 1–30 (HTML, CSS, layout, and Tailwind) are warm-up levels — work through them at your own pace; nothing is saved or graded. Once you finish Level 30 and enter the JavaScript phase, every level from Level 31 onward is recorded in your browser, scored with a 10-question quiz, and gates access to the next level until you pass.

Welcome to your full-stack journey

This system guides you through 115 levels that take you from absolute beginner to professional full-stack developer.

How this system works

Each level gives you two files:

  • level[number].html/js/php — instructions, preview, and navigation
  • example[number].html/js/php — the target design you need to recreate

Your mission: study the example, then recreate it using only the skills specified for that level.

Quiz system — Levels 31 and up

  • 10 questions per level
  • 2 minute time limit
  • 10/10 score required to proceed
  • Randomized question order on every attempt
  • Saved automatically to your browser's local storage
  • No skipping — the next level stays locked until you pass

Learning philosophy

This system follows a "learn by building" approach. Instead of just reading concepts, you'll immediately apply them by creating real components, applications, and full-stack projects.

Getting started

  1. Start with Level 1 — don't skip the basics, even if you think you know them.
  2. Create both files for each level (level.html and example.html).
  3. Study the example by opening it in your browser.
  4. Recreate it yourself using only the skills specified for that level.
  5. Pass the quiz (Levels 31+) to unlock the next level.
  6. Use navigation to move between levels systematically.
  7. Practice consistently — even one level a day builds real proficiency.
